Education and Experience

Alexander Spalding attended Oregon State University before travelling to attend the University of Illinois, College of Law on scholarship. During his time there he clerked for two judges before graduating cum laude with honors.

An Oregon Family

Originally growing up in Oregon, Alex and his wife, Missy, were high school sweethearts. Following his graduation from college he and his wife moved to Illinois during his time in law school, but returned home to what they believe is the most beautiful place in the United States: Oregon. They have 3 children, and when he is not in the office, they enjoy all of the adventures of central Oregon.
